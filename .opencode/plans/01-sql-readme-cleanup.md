# LeetCode SQL Practice

The LeetCode SQL questions are meant to help you gain confidence and fluency with SQL. Reading about SQL or doing a few lab exercises won't build the permanent learning you need — consistent practice will. The goal is to work through all 77 free SQL problems until you can consistently solve "Easy" and some "Medium" questions without hints.

**Note:** Practicing SQL on LeetCode will help you think about how to join data on any platform, including NoSQL. Clear thinking about joins is a transferable skill that benefits you even in designing stateless applications.

---

## Problem List

Use the following link to find database problems to practice on and solve:

https://leetcode.com/problem-list/2hv17fjt

### Study Tips

- Try resetting problems and solving them without looking up syntax or solutions
- Practice with a timer to simulate real interview conditions
- You can ask AI for help and look at others' solutions while learning
- Focus on eventually solving problems independently

# In-Person SQL Interviews

The in-person SQL interviews are a great way to practice what you'll actually experience in real job interviews. Being able to explain your SQL thinking out loud is a totally different skill than solving problems quietly at your computer. These mock interviews give you a chance to practice communicating your approach, handling feedback, and thinking on your feet in a supportive environment. You can retake the interview as many times as you'd like until you get the score you want!

**Note:** In-person interviews are strongly preferred. The **Medium Without Hints Interview is REQUIRED to be done in person.**

---

## Submission Instructions

Submit a **text entry** on Canvas containing the following self-evaluation:

1. **Interview details**
   - Who did you interview with?
   - When did the interview take place?

2. **Self-rating (1–3 points)**
   - Rate your interview performance using the following guidelines:
     1 - Poor performance, unprepared, but you did show up!
     2 - Good performance, eventually got the right answer!
     3 - Excellent performance, impressive work, well done!

3. **LeetCode submission URL**
   - Copy and paste your submission link using this format:
     https://leetcode.com/problems/**some-problem**/submissions/**your_submission_id**/

   - Example:
     https://leetcode.com/problems/equal-row-and-column-pairs/submissions/970584228/

   - **Make sure it is the submission link and not any other format as this is the only one that works. Otherwise, you may not receive credit**
